OPC INDUSTRIES

Mobile/web development and XP/Lean consulting for good causes

Optimize for personal happiness

  • Take on interesting work over high paid work
  • Don't take on work that won't pay the bills
  • Allocate sufficient time for personal life and creative pursuits

Only work on good products

  • Contribute towards the UN's Sustainable Development Goals
  • Contribute to the sustainability or democratisation of the arts (literature, theater, music etc)

Minimize carbon footprint

  • Offset air travel
  • Travel by train instead of air wherever possible (even if this is slower and requires stopovers)
  • Don't replace existing tools unless they seriously impede work or become a security risk
